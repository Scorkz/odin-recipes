Lasagna

Description

This is a recipe for creating the worlds best Lasagna that'll make all your friend jealous at your cooking prowess!

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato products
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna accoriding to the recipe instructions
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving
