Potato Soup

Description

This is a recipe for creating Potato Soup that's perfect for those cozy snow days!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound bacon
  • 1 large onion, diced
  • 8 potatoes, diced
  • 1 tablespoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon ground black pepper
  • 3 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 3 cups water
  • 1 gallon whole milk

Steps

  1. Cook bacon until evenly brown
  2. Mix the onion and coat with the bacon drippings
  3. Stir in potatoe and season with salt and pepper
  4. Mix in the flour and water, stirring until smooth
  5. Cover pot and cook for 10 minutes, until potatoe are tender
  6. Recue heat to low and gradually stir in the milk
  7. Simmer soup for 1 hour, stirring frequently to avoid scorching
